Learning about Tom RESUME

Tom holds a PhD from Arizona State University, a Master of Information Systems Management (MISM) from Gonzaga University, and a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) from Gonzaga University. With a solid academic foundation, he has built an extensive career in higher education. Tom served as an Associate Professor at Gonzaga University, Assistant Professor at the University of Alabama and West Texas A&M University, and later as a Clinical Assistant Professor at Arizona State University. His technical expertise includes database design and development, entity-relationship diagramming, and proficiency in Structured Query Language (SQL). Tom is also skilled in using advanced data visualization tools such as Tableau and Excel, including Solver, PivotTables, and Charts. In addition to his strong analytical capabilities, Tom has a working knowledge of web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and is proficient in Flowchart diagramming and Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) programming in Excel.
